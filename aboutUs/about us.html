About us

We at Helping Hands, have created this platform where donor, receiver and hospital blood bank all come together at one portal. In this application receiver can directly come in contact with the donors and volunteers of helping hands within the area if there is any shortage of blood in the blood bank/Hospital.


We believe that by automating the existing manual blood bank management system we can bring all the donor,receiver,hospital and blood bank together at one portal and their needs are satisfied.
